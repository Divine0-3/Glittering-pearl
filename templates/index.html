Pearl Care Guide

Keep your pearls looking beautiful for generations

Cleaning

Clean your pearls with a soft, damp cloth after each wear to remove oils and perspiration. Never use harsh chemicals or ultrasonic cleaners.

Storage

Store pearls separately in a soft pouch or lined jewelry box. Keep them away from other jewelry to prevent scratches and maintain their luster.

Metal Care

Our pearls and gemstones are naturally durable, and the metal components are crafted with quality materials. A gentle wipe with a soft cloth keeps them looking their best.

Daily Wear

Enjoy wearing your pearls and gemstones with confidence. They're designed for everyday elegance, making them perfect companions for any occasion.
